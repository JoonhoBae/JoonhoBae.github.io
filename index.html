About

My research seeks to develop data-driven approaches to analyze, predict, and control complex systems based on machine learning and Bayesian statistical methodologies. I have an interdisciplinary training backgroud including statistics, industrial engineering, and financial economics. The main research topics include content industries (mobile games, social science, mobile log data analysis).

I am a part of SILAB(Systems Intelligence Lab.) led by my advisor Jinkyoo Park.
